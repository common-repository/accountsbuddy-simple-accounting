AccountsBuddy is an awesome WordPress plugin which can help you manage your business accounts easily through WordPress dashboard and keep track of expenses and important transactions.

== Description ==
AccountsBuddy WordPress plugins is a helpful WordPress accounting plugin to manage your company or business accounts right from your WordPress dashboard. So if you are looking for a simple way to manage general ledger or keep the journal of daily trnsactions you have found the right WordPress plugin to help you manage your business accounts.
The double entry accounting system helps you to easily move transactions from various accounts. We are excited to announce the start of new era in WordPress with awesome accounts management WordPress plugin.

Important Features
1. Add unlimited companies
2. Each company have its own accounts
3. Accounts types and accounts groups help you separate different types of accounts
4. Journal Voucher to add entries of your business transactions
5. Various types of reports helps you better understand business requirements
6. Print the single voucher or report

== Installation ==

Install plugin to your WordPress then follow steps below.

1. Go to Accounts Buddy menu in your WordPress dashboard
2. Create a company and then add accounts to that company
3. Now you are ready to add Journal Vouchers or transactions to those accounts
4. Generate reports and you are good
